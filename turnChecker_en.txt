Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 15, 2026

Turn Checker is an iOS application designed to help users plan and manage turns in strategy games such as 4X games and wargames.
The app allows users to enter and manage game-related information (for example game names, turn schedules and notes). This information is stored locally on the user’s device.

If the user uses iCloud, this data may be synced across the user’s Apple devices using Apple’s iCloud services.
The developer of the app does not collect, access, or store user data on external servers.
